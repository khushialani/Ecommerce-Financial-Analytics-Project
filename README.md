# Ecommerce-Financial-Analytics-Project
📌 Project Overview

This project performs an end-to-end financial analytics study on e-commerce transaction data to evaluate business performance.

The analysis focuses on:

Revenue trend analysis & forecasting

Customer churn identification

Profitability evaluation

Financial risk assessment

Strategic business recommendations

The goal is to convert raw transactional data into actionable business insights that support data-driven decision-making.

🎯 Business Objectives

Analyze historical revenue growth and seasonality

Forecast future revenue trends

Identify customer churn patterns and retention gaps

Evaluate profitability drivers across categories and payment behavior

Provide strategic recommendations to improve financial performance

🛠 Tech Stack

Python

Pandas – Data manipulation

NumPy – Numerical computation

Matplotlib / Seaborn – Data visualization

Scikit-learn – Modeling

Time Series Forecasting (ARIMA / Prophet) (if used)

Google Colab

📊 Project Workflow
1️⃣ Data Cleaning & Preparation

Handled missing values

Converted date columns into proper datetime format

Created monthly revenue aggregations

Engineered features for churn detection

2️⃣ Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Monthly revenue trend analysis

Category-wise revenue contribution

Seller-level revenue distribution

Installment payment analysis

3️⃣ Revenue Forecasting

Built time-series forecasting model

Identified seasonal trends

Predicted future revenue trajectory

Evaluated model performance

4️⃣ Customer Churn Analysis

Defined churn based on inactivity window

Identified high-risk customer segments

Evaluated impact of churn on revenue stability

5️⃣ Profitability Analysis

Compared revenue vs payment patterns

Analyzed high-margin vs low-margin segments

Identified revenue concentration risks

📈 Key Insights

Revenue shows consistent growth with noticeable seasonal spikes.

A small percentage of sellers contribute a large portion of total revenue (revenue concentration risk).

Customer churn significantly impacts long-term revenue sustainability.

Installment-based transactions influence profit margins.

Certain product categories drive disproportionately higher profitability.

💡 Business Recommendations

Implement targeted retention campaigns to reduce churn.

Diversify revenue dependency by supporting mid-tier sellers.

Optimize installment policies to protect margins.

Increase investment in high-performing product categories.

Improve operational efficiency to enhance customer satisfaction.
